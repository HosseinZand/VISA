Exit

by Yixin Cao

Yixin was described environment pollution is happening on our earth home. She took some inspiration from photographer Alberto Seveso, who captured clouds of ink underwater. Therefore, she decided to use ink rendering is a metaphor for air chemical pollution. She injected the acrylic paint into the water with a syringe needle. The acrylic paint like clouds in the water, slowly covered the village, the woods, giving a sense of suffocation. About this ink rendering projection, could capture the audience's interaction and move their feelings. There is the collision of two primary colours of yellow and blue, and the fusion of complementary colours of yellow and purple. Besides, the mixture of colours creates a chaotic atmosphere that covers the houses and woods like human beings surrounded by chemical gases. People use more natural resources, the earth will wear out faster, and our homeland will disappear in future. I hope that this project of my art will bring people to think about the ecological environment needs in future.

Polluted Sea

by Juyeong Kang

My concept of this video art is polluted sea.In the present era where we live, environmental pollution is serious the earth in the middle also represents polluted seas worldwide.In particular, a lot of trash thrown into the sea. My materials were used as recyclable or non-recycling waste which is common around us.The reason why i chose these materials are used is because of the largest amount of trash thrown into the sea such as plastic, paper.Viewers who see my work able to see my work as pollution of the sea and feel how serious ocean pollution is in our time.

Micocosmos To World

by Xiaozhen

This is focusing on the Medical Microcosm, I want to turn the whole video's feeling into a mysterious and melancholic environment by using color bluish-green and blue. Also, I want to talk about video projection in a glass container to imitate a larger version of the container to store these microscopic creatures, because in real life these tiny things can only be seen through a microscope. I found some related videos from the website, using Premiere Pro to collage them. The video is from the beginning of the process of dividing the fertilized egg, which shows that people grow from the initial cells. These short videos are used to show that after we grow up, life is composed of different things. Although their size is very small, they are still important.
