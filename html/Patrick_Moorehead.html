Shrine of Cleanliness

Patrick Moorehead

I wanted to create a 3D object or objects that both resembled a previous project in which I created a video that showed how to wash ones hands, as well as a piece that speaks about what is currently going on in todays society. With the coronavirus gripping our lives and forcing us to stay inside, I wanted to make a piece that showed one of the mantras we all have seemed to take very seriously: washing our hands. Something that was once so mundane and every day is now at the forefront of our lives.
