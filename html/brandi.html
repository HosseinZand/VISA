Our museum, Into a New Reality, is a gallery of overall magical themes. Inside the domed building, you'll find a hallway leading to two galleries that contain our 2D works, and a main room housing our main 3D element, a tree. The ancient idea of a new reality, similar, but not quite like the one we reside in. We were inspired by fantasy, magic, and postapocalyptic themes.
